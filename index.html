Your party is battered at the end of an adventuring day, with two allies captured inside. **Tonight’s objective (this session):** - **Extract Lenore** from the locked Infirmary (14) and secure a safe **Long Rest Chamber**. - **Extract BattleBeast** from the Pens (5). - Escape alive. **Next session hooks (do not fully resolve tonight):** - **Maverick** is held upstairs for interrogation by **General Varkus**. - **Shadowhigh** is in the dungeon below, scheduled for hanging at dawn unless events change. --- # Background & Political Fault Lines Waterdeep is not a frontier town, and it hates being treated like one. ### The Factions **General Varkus (Imperial Governor).** Wants a strong, multinational empire with a hard military core—an echo of **Zanzibar** (this world’s Caesar). Varkus is courting Waterdeep’s old-money elite and ancient trades—**a new development**. **The Unmasked Lord ap Dyksterhaus.** Openly flaunts imperial customs, rails against overtaxation, and champions merchant interests. His base is the merchant class (and those who profit from them). **The Old Houses.** Ancient wealth and older magic. They despise the merchant class but love imperial peace, trade routes, and access. Varkus courting them is a tectonic shift. **The Farmlands.** Revolts flare beyond the city over imperial seizure of grain and cattle. ### Recent Incidents (Tonight) - A **foiled heist at Tiffany House**, where Varkus was raising funds and making friends among the elite. - A violent incident at **Ard Farkle’s**: a convict later held in the fort fired into bystanders; **carts burned and horses died**. - The vernal equinox approaches. The parties are thinner. The city’s smiles feel rented. --- # Running the Fort This module is built to avoid “one bad roll = TPK.” It’s a pressure system. ## Zone Alerts (Localized) Track alert separately for each zone: - **Zone A:** Exterior/Gate (1–2) - **Zone B:** Courtyard & Arcade (6–9) - **Zone C:** Service & Command (10–13) - **Zone D:** Lower Halls (14–22) Each zone has **Alert 0–3**: - **0 Normal:** routine patrols. - **1 Suspicious:** doubled checks, a runner dispatched. - **2 Active Search:** doors watched, patrols tighten. - **3 Lockdown:** doors barred, reinforcements stage. **Fort-wide Lockdown** only happens if the **Dispatch Horn** in (11) is sounded. ## Keys (Two Rings) **Ring A (on wall pegboard in 11):** Infirmary (14), Back Hall (16), Dungeon stairs (20). **Ring B (on Colonel Angus):** Stairs Up (12), Alarm Cabinet, upstairs protocols. **Key Visibility:** the pegboard is visible from the doorway of (11). Ring B is clipped to Angus’ sword belt. ## Stairs Up Are Inaccessible Tonight The **stairs up (12)** are: - Locked (Ring B) - Under **Arcane Lock** from above (War Wizard protocol) - **Barred from the top** (physical bar) Even with Ring B, opening (12) requires upstairs access. This keeps tonight focused. --- # Rumors & Overheard Talk (Use as Breadcrumbs) Roll or pick when PCs pause to listen. {{twoCol | d8 | Rumor | |---:|---| | 1 | “Dyksterhaus says the Emperor bleeds merchants dry while nobles dine.” | | 2 | “Varkus wants Zanzibar’s kind of unity—steel first, then peace.” | | 3 | “The old houses like imperial peace.
